COMPANY PROFILE

We are handling import, export, indenting of Prime & Secondary quality Steel & iron Sheet and coils, Long and flat products, Stainless steel of S200, S400 and S300 Magnetic, Austenitic, Martensitic grades of JIS,AISI ASTM Etc. standard. Also we do Ferrous & non-Ferrous Scraps and ores.

The company office is located in the main trading ,retail and whole sale hub in old Dhaka under south city corporation, Holding address No.1, Nazira Bazar(1st floor), Majed Sarder Road ,Bangshal,Dhaka-1100 Bangladesh.
